GlamSync: POS & Inventory Management System

A comprehensive desktop-based solution for Miss Ivee Browsthetic Salon

Project Overview

GlamSync is a desktop-based inventory management and point-of-sale (POS) system specifically designed for Miss Ivee Browsthetic Salon. The name "GlamSync" is derived from "Glamorous Synchronization" - representing the perfect blend of beauty and functionality. The term "glamorous" reflects sophistication, style, and elegance, while "synchronization" represents the coordination of inventory management and POS features working in harmony.

This capstone project was developed by a team of four BSIT students from STI College Lucena (Shyrine Ann C. Chong, Kyla Sandra R. Millor, Clarence Josh S. Ricafrente, and Princess Michaela S. Torres) and completed in December 2025.

Problem Statement

Miss Ivee Browsthetic Salon, established in 2019 in Lucena City, faced significant operational challenges due to their reliance on manual processes:

  • Manual Inventory Tracking: Staff used Excel spreadsheets and paper-based records to track products, which was time-consuming and error-prone
  • Inaccurate Stock Management: Difficulty tracking used items and needed supplies due to the demands of the busy work environment
  • Point-of-Sale Issues: Manual transaction recording led to miswritten payment amounts, resulting in cash discrepancies
  • Sales Report Errors: Manual calculations often contained mistakes, making it difficult to track business performance accurately
  • Limited Scalability: The manual system couldn't keep up with the salon's growth from their original location to their current expanded facility

The Solution: GlamSync System

GlamSync was developed to address these challenges through automation and integration. The system provides a comprehensive solution that streamlines inventory management and sales operations while maintaining accuracy and efficiency.

System Modules in Detail

1. Login & Authentication

The system implements a secure multi-tier authentication system with four account types:

  • Administrator (Owner): Full system access including account management
  • Manager: Access to all operational modules except account management
  • Cashier: Limited to POS and Appointment modules
  • Guest: Emergency backup account with cashier-level access

All accounts (except Guest) are protected with Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for enhanced security. Passwords are encrypted to ensure confidentiality.

Development Process

Phase 1: Planning

The development team conducted extensive interviews with Ms. Ivy Hervera (Owner) and Ms. Jalene Dela Cruz (Admin) to understand their operational challenges and requirements. Key findings included:

  • Need for automated inventory tracking
  • Requirement for accurate POS with receipt generation
  • Appointment scheduling functionality
  • Expense and sales reporting capabilities

Phase 2: Design

Using Canva, the team created comprehensive wireframes and blueprints for the system interface. The design phase emphasized user-friendliness, as many salon staff members were not highly tech-savvy.

System Design Wireframe
User Interface Blueprint

Phase 3: Development

Development began with setting up the environment using Visual Studio 2022 and SQL Server Management Studio. The team implemented:

  • Front-end: Windows Forms for UI design with interactive controls
  • Back-end: C# for business logic and database connectivity
  • Database: SQL Server for data storage and management

Phase 4: Testing

The testing phase included two stages:

  • Internal Testing: Three rounds of testing with the capstone adviser, identifying and resolving bugs, errors, and UI improvements
  • External Testing: Client testing by Ms. Ivy Hervera, validating functionality and gathering feedback for final adjustments

Phase 5: Implementation

The system was successfully deployed at Miss Ivee Browsthetic Salon with hands-on training for the owner and admin staff. All requested enhancements were implemented, including color scheme adjustments and the Account Management module.

Recommendations for Future Enhancement

While GlamSync successfully meets the current needs of Miss Ivee Browsthetic Salon, several enhancements could further improve the system:

1. Cloud Synchronization

Integrate an online database or cloud synchronization to allow multi-device access and real-time updates. This would be beneficial for larger salons with multiple workstations.

2. Backup and Recovery

Implement automatic data backup and restore functionality to protect important business data from accidental loss or system failure.

3. Hardware Integration

Explore integration with hardware devices such as receipt printers and barcode scanners to enable faster and more professional transaction processing.

4. Scalability Optimization

Optimize the system's scalability to handle larger data volumes and support more concurrent users for larger salon businesses.

5. Mobile Access

Develop a companion mobile application for appointment scheduling and basic monitoring capabilities.
